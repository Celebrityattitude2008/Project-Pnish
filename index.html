Safety Tips

  • Always check the URL carefully before clicking.
  • Look for HTTPS and a padlock symbol in your browser.
  • Avoid entering personal information on untrusted sites.
  • Use two-factor authentication for important accounts.
  • Be wary of unexpected email links requesting verification.
  • Use a reputable antivirus or browser protection tool.
