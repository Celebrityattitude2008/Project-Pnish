          Why these checks matter

          What is typosquatting?

          Attackers use look-alike domains such as paypa1.com or g00gle.com to trick users into believing the site is legitimate.

          Why are short links dangerous?

          Shorteners hide the destination, making it easier for phishing or malware pages to appear legitimate until it is too late.

          Why check WHOIS data?

          Freshly registered domains are often used by attackers because they have no reputation history yet.
